About MAPLE

Learn about MAPLE and its capabilities in molecular dynamics simulations.

Overview

MAPLE is a free and open-source software suite designed for high-performance molecular dynamics simulations and comprehensive output analysis. It provides researchers and scientists with powerful tools to study molecular systems at the atomic level.

Features

  • High-performance molecular dynamics simulations
  • Comprehensive analysis tools
  • Open-source and freely available
  • Extensive documentation and community support
